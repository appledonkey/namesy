FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 characters max - currently ~2100)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Finding the perfect name for your baby should be fun, not overwhelming. Namesy makes it easy to explore thousands of names, understand their meanings, and discover the one that feels just right.

★ SWIPE TO DISCOVER
Browse names Tinder-style. Swipe right on names you love, left on ones that aren't quite right. Your favorites are saved automatically so you can review them later with your partner.

★ DEEP NAME INSIGHTS
Every name includes:
• Origin and cultural background
• Meaning and history
• Popularity trends over time
• Common nicknames
• Similar names you might also like

★ SMART ANALYSIS
Our unique radar chart shows how each name scores across 8 dimensions:
• Uniqueness - Stand out or blend in?
• Timelessness - Classic or trendy?
• Pronunciation - Easy to say?
• Spelling - Easy to spell?
• Nickname potential - Fun short versions?
• Professional sound - Works in any career?
• Teasing resistance - Playground-proof?
• Flow - Sounds great with your last name?

★ NAME PREVIEW
See how first, middle, and last names look together. Try different fonts to visualize the full name in various styles.

★ WORKS OFFLINE
No internet? No problem. The entire database of 2,700+ names is included in the app. Perfect for browsing anywhere - doctor's waiting room, late-night brainstorming, or during your commute.

★ COMPLETELY PRIVATE
Your name choices are personal. Namesy stores everything locally on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no data collection. Your favorites stay yours.

Whether you're looking for something classic like Charlotte or unique like Zephyr, traditional or modern, popular or rare - Namesy helps you find the name that's meant to be.

Download now and start your naming journey!
